About Davis

I'm a Year 3 Psychology student at a Kenyan university, expected to graduate in 2027. My work bridges psychology, media, and technology — turning psychological concepts into accessible content for real people.

I host Psychology With Us, a podcast exploring African perspectives on mental health. I've built i-Personality, a web-based personality assessment tool. And I'm a published poet under the pen name Kieran Rivenhall.

My philosophy: psychology shouldn't live only in textbooks and therapy rooms. Every project I build reflects that belief.

🎤 Mental Health Talks

Structured presentations for schools, universities, corporates, and community groups. Topics: stress, emotional intelligence, personality, identity, relationships, and African psychology perspectives.

🛠️ Psychoeducation Workshops

Interactive group sessions teaching psychological concepts in practical, relatable ways. Covering coping skills, self-awareness, communication, and behavior change.

👥 Peer Support Facilitation

Facilitation of peer support circles, student wellness groups, and mental health awareness campaigns. Group dynamics trained.

🧩 Personality Assessment Consultancy

Help applying or building personality frameworks for teams, schools, or digital products. Test design, results interpretation, and organizational application.
